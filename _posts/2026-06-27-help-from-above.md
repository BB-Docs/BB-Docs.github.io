### Thematic Overview

Today's lessons form a single continuous arc. Each segment builds on the last:

1. **Help from Above** — The Creator constantly presses on the soul, alternating it between hardship and relief, to grow it toward the goal.
2. **Building the Vessel** — Through the environment and "above reason," a person constructs the vessel of Bestowal (Chafetz Chesed), moving from fear to love.
3. **The Bathhouse & Circumcision** — Feeling spiritually naked (honest self-examination) is the starting point. Circumcision — the unconditional covenant above reason — is the foundation to rebuild from.
4. **Na'aseh V'Nishma (We Will Do & We Will Hear)** — Act first as a pure messenger/angel (faith above reason), then come to hear/understand (revelation). This is the angelic secret Israel discovered at Sinai.
5. **The Good Fate** — Surrendering personal reason to the values of the environment is how the Creator places a person's hand on the good fate, bringing him to the degree of a naked messenger who rejoices in his portion.

## Lesson 1 — Help That Comes From Above

*Source: Igrot HaRanch (Letters of the Rabbi), Vol. 1, p. 193 | Lesson recorded February 17, 2002*

---

### What Is Help From Above?

Help from Above is anything that advances a person toward the purpose of creation — even if it comes in difficult or painful "clothing." Just as a teacher gives a student hard exercises so he grows, Divine help can arrive as hardship, failure, or burden, not only as comfort. Help (in either form) begins when a person is genuinely ready to overcome it and receive it beneficially.

---

### The Spiritual Journey — Stage by Stage

#### Stage 1: Starting Point — Person as Zero

A person begins as spiritual zero. The Creator wishes to elevate him to Kedushah (holiness) and gives him a desire whose origin, nature, and purpose he does not know.

#### Stage 2: Searching According to the Desire

- The desire instinctively pulls the person toward the right place.
- He moves from place to place, scrutinizing without knowing how.
- These scrutinies also come from Above.

#### Stage 3: Settling and Receiving a Method

Once settled, he receives a method. The help at this stage arrives as a burden — questions and difficulties. He must choose:

- **Run away** → leads back to the same suffering as before.
- **Advance** → he doesn't know how; requires more study and observation.

**Key realization: advancement comes only through asking the Creator for help.**

#### Stage 4: Crying Out and Receiving Help

A person goes through many cycles of burden, failure, and disappointment before genuinely turning to the Creator. Once he cries out, the Creator helps — the Creator is revealed, and the person gains the power to justify:

- The Left-Line help (heavy heart, bad desires, alien thoughts) — as a gift, not a punishment.
- The subsequent help from the Creator to overcome and move forward in spite of those thoughts.

#### Stage 5: The Cycle Repeats

Just when the person feels stable — on the "King's Highway," in control, standing on both feet — new alien thoughts, fears, anxieties, and lack of confidence arrive from Above. It seems like a setback. He enters depression and despair.

**Key insight:** Time passes. Continued study and exertion, even occasional, accumulates. Suffering + exertion together bring help from Above again. He rises and later sees that the questions themselves caused progress. This cycle repeats each time, at each level.

---

### Torah Comes Out of Zion

"The Torah comes out of Zion" — Zion (Tzion) shares a root with Yetziyah (exit). The Torah — spiritual growth — emerges from the exits and returns to Kedushah. A person is built through these repeated cycles of leaving and returning.

---

### How the Upper Light Works

The Upper Light ("Father of Creation") constantly presses on every soul. The soul contains two opposite forces:

- **Will to Receive** — from the soul's own nature.
- **Will to Bestow** — from the shuddering of the vessels; the point in the heart.

The Light's constant pressure alternates the soul between the Left Line (difficulty/receiving) and the Right Line (joy/bestowal). This is how it grows on both legs.

#### The Two Angels

The Creator sends one pressure — the Upper Light. The soul perceives it as two:

- The Good Angel / Good Inclination
- The Bad Angel / Evil Inclination

**Both operate in the same direction — toward the purpose of creation.**

---

### Levels of Concealment

**Double Concealment:** "I feel bad — that's it." The Creator cannot be sensed behind the difficulty at all.

**Single (Ordinary) Concealment:** "I feel bad, but I begin to sense this comes from Him." The Creator is hidden yet felt.

**Crossing the Barrier (Revelation):** After justifying and discovering the Creator from double to single concealment enough times, the Creator is revealed. This is crossing the Machsom.

The society — the friends — must constantly remind each person that everything happening around him comes from the Creator's thought of creation, to benefit His creations, and to bring him closer to the goal every moment.

## Lesson 2 — Building the Vessel: From Fear to Love

*Topic: From Binah to Masach — From Chafetz Chesed to Arvut*

---

### Phase 1 — The Vessel of Bestowal (Binah / Chafetz Chesed)

#### The Role of Environment

A person cannot correct himself through his own reason and feeling alone. The environment (society, friends) provides examples — values and actions above one's own intellect and emotion. Accepting these above one's own reason is the core practice:

- Act according to the society's values, in opposition to one's own ego.
- Both elements must be present: the society's example AND the individual's acceptance above his own understanding.

#### How the Light Is Invited

When a person acts by the environment's values rather than his own calculations, his actions align with the Light. Through the network of connection within the group, the Light is invited in and begins to influence him.

#### The Vessel — Chafetz Chesed / Milah

Gradually a vessel called "Above Reason," "Chafetz Chesed" (Delighted in Mercy), or "Milah" (Circumcision) forms in the person. This vessel operates only through Bestowal:

- Mind and feelings no longer belong to the self.
- The person becomes like a machine, angel, or messenger — no personal calculations.
- "What's in the package? Who's it from? Who is it for? No calculations!"

**This is the first degree: Mutual Guarantee (Arvut) / Mutual Responsibility.**

---

### Phase 2 — Repentance Out of Love

After building the vessel through Chafetz Chesed to Masach (screen) and Arvut, the person wants to go beyond mere passive transmission:

- **Phase 1 (Fear):** He is a messenger — delivers what is given to him.
- **Phase 2 (Love):** He becomes an active conduit — draws Light and delivers it; receives and attains for others.

#### Revealing Hochmah

In the phase of love, the person reveals the illumination of Hochmah (wisdom). He passes it through the network built on the degree of Binah, filling others with everything he can receive for them. In this work he begins to feel the purpose of creation and to know the Creator.

**Final realization:** "Today, you would apply to your heart that the Lord is your God — nothing else besides Him." In keeping the mutual guarantee, the person becomes responsible for others.

## Lesson 3 — The Bathhouse, Nakedness & Circumcision

*Source: Rabash — article on King David in the bathhouse*

---

### The Bathhouse — Honest Self-Examination

Rabash uses the image of King David entering a bathhouse to describe a painful inner spiritual moment. The bathhouse represents a state of purity in which a person examines himself honestly:

- He looks at his past Torah and Mitzvot and cannot honestly say any of it was done truly for the Creator.
- He looks at the present and finds he doesn't want to do anything either.
- He sees himself as completely naked — spiritually exposed and empty.

> *Rabash: "He sees himself as naked... woe unto me, for I am standing naked without Mitzvot."*

---

### Circumcision — The Foundation Above Reason

What rescues David in that moment is remembering the circumcision. In its spiritual meaning, circumcision is not a physical memory — it is the covenant that exists above reason, feeling, and self-calculation:

- The infant has no awareness during circumcision — there are no foreign thoughts, no ego involved.
- It represents an unconditional commitment that precedes understanding.
- Something in the person already belongs to the Creator regardless of his current state.

**The result:** From this foundation — knowing the covenant exists above his feelings — David's mind is eased. He begins to rebuild his order of work on the basis of "above reason."

**Practical implication for the ten:** When a person feels spiritually naked and empty, the answer is not to find reasons or feelings to continue — it is to remember that the circumcision (the unconditional commitment) already exists. From there, the work above reason can be rebuilt.

## Lesson 4 — Na'aseh V'Nishma: We Will Do & We Will Hear

*Source: Rabash — article on the declaration at Sinai*

---

### The Reversed Order

The Israelites at Sinai declared: "Na'aseh V'Nishma" — "We will do and we will hear." This reverses ordinary logic, in which one first understands and then acts. Spiritually, the order is: act first, then come to understand. This astonished even the angels — it was as if Israel had discovered the angels' own secret.

---

### The Two Levels

#### "We Will Do" — The Angel / Messenger Stage

An angel is purely a messenger. He has zero personal interest in the package:

- What's in it, who sent it, who receives it — none of this concerns him.
- His only pleasure is in the act of serving — carrying out the King's will.
- This is acting above reason: doing without needing to understand, without personal calculation.

**This stage = Faith above reason.**

#### "We Will Hear" — The Receiver Stage

At this higher level, the person is no longer a neutral messenger. He now:

- Understands the gift fully — knows what is in the package.
- Grasps the importance of what the Giver is sending.
- Experiences the direct relationship between giver and receiver.

**This stage = Revelation — "The Lord is your God."**

---

### Practical Meaning for the Ten

The question for each person right now, in this moment, in this group: Can I operate as an angel? Can I act for the sake of the ten without calculating what is in it for me, without needing to understand first? The understanding — "we will hear" — comes afterward, as a reward for the doing.

Rabash: The meaning of "apply to your heart" is We Will Do — faith above reason. Afterwards comes the reward: "the Lord is your God" — We Will Hear.

## Lesson 5 — The Good Fate & The Naked Messenger

---

### What Is the Good Fate?

The Creator places a person's hand on the "good fate." The good fate is the point at which a person begins connecting with the correct environment and receiving from it spiritual values — the ability to work according to what the environment defines as good.

---

### My Values vs. The Environment's Values

Operating according to one's own emotions and intellect works against spiritual progress:

- Personal emotions and intellect do not lead toward spirituality or the goal.
- They keep a person locked in his own calculations and the will to receive.

Operating according to the values of the environment — by constantly connecting to it and checking one's own values against it — brings the person to the good fate.

---

### The Creator's Guidance

The Creator places a person's hand on the good fate and says: "Take it — my entire choice, my entire scrutiny, every step along the way is here." This is an invitation to surrender personal calculation and accept the environment's authority as one's guide.

**The measure of it:** The extent to which I subdue myself and accept the authority and reason of the society over myself. Once I decide to accept them, I accept them always — I become like a flock that follows them.

---

### The Degree: Naked Messenger Who Rejoices in His Portion

This complete surrender of personal reason to the environment's values brings a person to the degree described across all of today's lessons:

- **Naked** — having stripped away all personal calculation and self-interest (the bathhouse).
- **Messenger** — acting as a pure conduit with no stake in the package (Na'aseh V'Nishma).
- **Who rejoices in his portion** — content with exactly what the Creator sends, with no demand for more or less (Chafetz Chesed).

*This is the point where all the threads of today's lessons converge.*
